Understanding the Client's Needs

Cybrosys Technologies strives to deliver solutions tailored to meet the unique needs of each client. After a thorough discussion and analysis, we have understood that the client requires an Odoo-based solution that is adaptable, scalable, and integrated seamlessly with their existing business processes.

The client aims to improve operational efficiency, enhance reporting capabilities, and ensure a smooth integration between different departments, including Sales, Inventory, Manufacturing, and Accounting. A key requirement is the ability to manage workflows more effectively and enable greater collaboration across teams.

Key Functional Areas

Based on our discussions, we have identified the following key functional areas for the Odoo implementation:

  • Sales Management: The client requires customizations in the sales workflow to ensure that quotes, orders, and invoices are efficiently processed. They need a system that can handle complex pricing rules, discounts, and commission tracking.
  • Inventory and Warehouse Management: A crucial need is the ability to track inventory levels in real-time, automate stock reordering, and improve stock movement tracking. The client also needs advanced reporting on warehouse performance and stock valuation.
  • Manufacturing: The client requires Odoo’s manufacturing module to be customized to better manage production scheduling, bill of materials, work orders, and product lifecycle management. The goal is to minimize lead times and enhance production efficiency.
  • Accounting and Finance: Odoo’s accounting system needs to be configured to meet the client’s tax regulations, multi-currency support, and financial reporting requirements. This will ensure smooth integration with other departments and allow for detailed financial analysis.
  • Human Resources: The client needs the HR module to be customized to handle recruitment, attendance, payroll, and performance evaluations. They are also looking for enhanced reporting and analytics on employee data.
  • Reporting and Analytics: The client requires advanced reporting capabilities that allow easy access to performance metrics, sales data, inventory analysis, and financial reports. Custom dashboards and reports need to be integrated into the system for better decision-making.

Custom Features and Integrations

The client also requires the following custom features and integrations to ensure that their business processes are fully supported:

  • Custom Workflows: Automation of business processes through custom workflows, ensuring that each department operates in sync and tasks are completed promptly.
  • Third-Party Integrations: Integration with external applications such as CRM systems, payment gateways, and email marketing platforms to streamline communication and data flow across systems.
  • Mobile Access: The client requires mobile access to key features, enabling employees to stay productive while on the go. Custom mobile interfaces will be developed to support field sales and warehouse staff.
  • Custom Notifications: Setting up customized alerts and notifications for key events, such as low stock levels, upcoming deadlines, or approval requirements. This will help ensure that stakeholders stay informed and proactive.

Implementation Approach

We will follow a structured approach to the implementation of the Odoo system:

  • Requirement Gathering: Our team will conduct detailed sessions with the client to understand their existing processes, identify pain points, and finalize system specifications.
  • System Customization: Based on the gathered requirements, we will proceed with configuring and customizing the Odoo modules to align with the client’s needs. This will include developing custom features and workflows as per the agreed specifications.
  • Testing and QA: We will perform extensive testing to ensure that the customizations are functioning as expected. This includes functional testing, user acceptance testing, and performance testing to ensure the system meets the client’s quality standards.
  • Training and Support: Post-implementation, we will provide training to the client’s staff to ensure smooth adoption of the system. Ongoing support will be available to address any issues or queries that arise.
  • Go-Live and Post-Implementation Review: After the system goes live, we will monitor its performance, make any necessary adjustments, and conduct a post-implementation review to ensure the solution meets the client's expectations.

Technical Configuration

Odoo Version: Odoo 18
Hosting Platform: Odoo.sh

Client-Supplied Details

The client has confirmed that their ERP infrastructure is built on the latest Odoo 18 platform, taking advantage of improved performance, updated backend capabilities, and new functional enhancements. The system is hosted on Odoo.sh, which offers a fully managed and scalable cloud environment optimized for development and continuous delivery.

The current setup includes both staging and production environments, integrated with GitHub for version control and deployment tracking. This infrastructure supports agile implementation workflows, allowing rapid testing and seamless delivery of new features or custom modules.

Access & Repository Details

The client has provided full access to their GitHub repository linked to the Odoo.sh instance. This includes permissions for pushing new branches, committing feature-specific code, and configuring automated staging builds. Access to staging and production databases has also been shared for testing, debugging, and module validation.

Development activities will be managed through feature branches, with each change undergoing validation in the staging environment before moving to production. This ensures stability, minimizes downtime, and maintains traceability throughout the implementation process.

Environment Readiness & Strategy

Based on the hosting architecture and client requirements, the solution will be developed using modular, upgrade-safe coding practices. Automated backup features, integrated logs, and rollback capabilities of Odoo.sh will be leveraged throughout the implementation cycle. Module Overview

Based on our understanding of the client’s operational structure and strategic objectives, the following core modules have been identified for implementation:

  • Sales
  • CRM (Customer Relationship Management)
  • Purchase
  • Inventory
  • Accounting & Finance
  • HRM (Human Resource Management)
  • Project Management

Sales Management

The Sales module will be configured to manage the complete sales lifecycle — from quotation to order confirmation and delivery. Key features include:

  • Quotation and Sales Order management
  • Dynamic product pricing and discount rules
  • Integration with inventory for real-time stock updates
  • Automated invoice generation from confirmed orders
  • Reporting dashboards for sales performance and pipeline forecasting

Salespersons will have access to lead timelines, follow-up schedules, and instant visibility into customer history, improving response time and client engagement.

CRM (Customer Relationship Management)

The CRM module will be implemented to enhance lead tracking, customer communication, and opportunity conversion. Functionality includes:

  • Lead generation through website forms and email campaigns
  • Custom pipeline stages for opportunity tracking
  • Activity scheduling and auto-reminder notifications
  • Internal team communication and customer interaction logs
  • Real-time conversion rate analytics and lost reason tagging

Purchase Management

The Purchase module will streamline procurement operations, reduce manual intervention, and support vendor collaboration. Key features include:

  • Purchase Request and RFQ generation
  • Vendor pricelist management
  • Automated PO creation based on stock rules
  • Three-way matching (PO, Invoice, Receipt)
  • Approval workflows for high-value purchases

Supplier performance metrics and lead time tracking will be incorporated to support better procurement decisions.

Inventory Management

The Inventory module will be configured to ensure accurate stock visibility, efficient transfers, and fulfillment automation. Key features include:

  • Warehouse and location hierarchy setup
  • Product categories, units of measure, and tracking methods
  • Incoming and outgoing shipment management
  • Internal transfers and delivery scheduling
  • Stock valuation and real-time availability updates
  • Batch, lot, and serial number tracking (if applicable)

Accounting & Finance

A localized accounting system will be implemented in accordance with the client’s chart of accounts, tax policies, and compliance requirements. Core functionalities include:

  • Automated journal entries from sales, purchases, and payments
  • Tax configuration (GST/VAT/Other as applicable)
  • Bank synchronization and reconciliation
  • Customer/vendor invoicing and payment tracking
  • Multi-currency management and fiscal year setup
  • Financial reports: Balance Sheet, P&L, Cash Flow, and Audit Logs

HRM (Human Resource Management)

The HR module will centralize employee records, streamline payroll, and enable attendance & leave tracking. Planned features include:

  • Employee profile and contract management
  • Leave request, approval flows, and accrual policies
  • Attendance integration (manual or biometric if applicable)
  • Department and job structure setup
  • Payroll configuration (basic salary + allowances + deductions)

The HRM system will help automate HR processes and maintain transparency in workforce operations.

Project Management

The Project module will support structured task planning, team coordination, and deadline tracking. It will include:

  • Project and sub-task hierarchy
  • Kanban and Gantt views for activity tracking
  • Time logging and progress status
  • Project budgeting and timesheet integration
  • Internal collaboration and document attachments

This module will assist in organizing deliverables, aligning teams, and ensuring on-time execution of key initiatives.

Additional Custom Requirements

The following requirements have been identified as custom needs beyond standard Odoo functionality. These will be developed specifically for the client:

  • Customized reports tailored to management KPIs
  • Third-party system integration via REST API
  • Role-based dashboards with department-specific views
  • Custom workflow for internal approvals
  • Mobile-responsive interface optimization for field users

Non-Functional Requirements

In addition to functional scope, the following non-functional requirements are important to ensure solution reliability and sustainability:

  • Performance: System should support 50+ concurrent users with minimal latency
  • Security: Role-based access, encrypted backups, and audit logs enabled
  • Scalability: Infrastructure should allow scale-up to support multi-branch operations
  • Maintainability: Codebase follows Odoo best practices and is version controlled
  • Uptime: System should maintain Auge availability under normal conditions
